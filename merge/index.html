About Nexus 2048

Nexus 2048 is a free merge puzzle game you can play right in your browser — no download, no signup. Slide the tiles in any direction; when two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one. Keep combining to reach the 2048 tile, then push for an even higher score. Easy to learn, hard to put down. Works on desktop and mobile, and your best score is saved.
