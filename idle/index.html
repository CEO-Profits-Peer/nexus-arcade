About Nexus Realms

Nexus Realms is a free idle action-RPG you play right in your browser — no download, no signup. Tap to strike monsters, hire companions that fight automatically, and travel a world map of eight regions, each tougher and richer than the last. Slay enemies and bosses to loot weapons and armor in four rarities, then equip and upgrade your gear to crit harder, burn foes and boost your gold and XP. When the grind slows, ascend for permanent Nexus Shards. Your progress saves automatically and keeps earning while you're away.
