About Nexus Run 3D

Nexus Run 3D is a free 3D endless runner you can play right in your browser — no download, no signup. Sprint down a glowing neon tunnel, switch between three lanes, jump over obstacles and scoop up energy orbs. The speed keeps climbing, so your reflexes have to keep up. Beat your distance record and challenge your friends. Runs on desktop and mobile.
