About Nexus Blocks

Nexus Blocks is a free falling-blocks puzzle game in the classic style, right in your browser — no download, no signup. Move and rotate the seven neon pieces to fill complete horizontal lines; each line you complete clears and scores points, and clearing several at once scores even more. The pieces fall faster as you level up, so plan ahead and keep the stack low. Works on desktop (keyboard) and mobile (touch buttons).
