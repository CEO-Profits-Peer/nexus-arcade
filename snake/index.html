About Nexus Snake

Nexus Snake is a free take on the timeless snake arcade game, right in your browser — no download, no signup. Guide your glowing snake to eat orbs and grow longer, but don't crash into the walls or your own tail. The longer you get, the faster it moves. Chase a new highscore and challenge your friends. Works on desktop and mobile.
