About Nexus Racer

Nexus Racer is a free endless car racing game you play right in your browser — no download, no signup. Weave through neon night traffic, collect coins, and build up your nitro to blast forward for extra distance. The further you drive, the faster it gets. Beat your personal record and challenge your friends. Works on desktop and mobile.
