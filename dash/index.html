About Nexus Dash

Nexus Dash is a free online arcade game you can play instantly in your browser — no download, no install, no signup. Guide your glowing node through an endless field of hazards, collect energy orbs to build your score multiplier, and push for a new personal highscore. It works on desktop and mobile.

How to play

Move your node with the mouse (desktop) or by dragging your finger (mobile). Avoid the red shards — a single hit ends your run. Collect the cyan orbs to raise your multiplier and score faster. The longer you survive, the faster and more crowded it gets. Share your score and challenge a friend to beat it.
