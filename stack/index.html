About Nexus Stack

Nexus Stack is a free timing and precision game you play right in your browser — no download, no signup. A glowing block slides across the top; drop it as perfectly as you can onto the growing tower. Any part that hangs over the edge gets sliced off, so nail your timing to keep your blocks wide. Perfect drops even earn a little width back. It gets faster the higher you climb. How tall can your neon tower get?
