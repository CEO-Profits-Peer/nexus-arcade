About Nexus Words

Nexus Words is a free daily word puzzle you can play right in your browser — no download, no signup. Every day there's one new 5-letter word to guess in six tries. Green means the letter is correct and in the right spot, yellow means it's in the word but in a different spot, and gray means it isn't in the word. Solve it, keep your streak alive, and share your result with friends. There's a fresh puzzle every day in both English and German.
