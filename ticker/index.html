About Nexus Ticker

Nexus Ticker is a free trading simulator you play right in your browser — no download, no signup. Start with $1,000 and 75 seconds on the clock, across four markets: a steady bond, two mid-risk stocks and one wild, high-volatility coin. Buy into dips, ride the news spikes, and sell before a crash wipes you out. When the bell rings, every position is cashed out automatically. A quarter of any profit also flows into your Nexus Finance Empire capital. How big can you grow your net worth?
