About Nexus Breaker

Nexus Breaker is a free neon brick breaker (breakout) game you play right in your browser — no download, no signup. Slide your paddle to bounce the ball, smash through walls of glowing bricks, and catch falling power-ups like multi-ball and a wider paddle. Clear a level to jump to a tougher one, and keep your score climbing. Works on desktop and mobile.
